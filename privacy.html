Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Videomera is a local-first utility for merging video clips. The app is designed to process selected files locally without sending your videos or XML manifests to us.

Data collection

Videomera does not collect, sell, or share personal data. It does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Files you select

Videomera can access only the files and folders you choose through system file pickers or drag and drop. This may include video files and supported XML manifests used for export naming and organization.

Local processing

Video merging and metadata parsing are performed locally on your device. Videomera does not upload your selected videos or manifests to a Videomera server.

Cloud-synced folders

If you choose an export destination inside iCloud Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive, or another synced folder, that service may upload or sync the finished file according to its own settings and privacy policy. Videomera does not control those services.

Support email

If you contact support, we will receive the information you choose to include in your email. Please avoid sending sensitive video files unless specifically requested.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to eraappai@gmail.com.
